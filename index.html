Making a Cream Pasta

Try our simplest recipe to make a delicious bacon cream pasta at your home that takes less than 10 minutes to make. You will not regret it!

Things You Need:

  • 1 Egg yolk
  • 400mL of Milk
  • 3 Bacon strips
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and Olive oil
  • 90g Spaghetti
  • 4 Peperoncino
  • Parmesan cheese

Step 1

Pour 400mL of milk into a bowl or a cup

Step 2

Put an egg yolk into a 400mL of milk

Step 3

Stir milk until an egg yolk is fully disolved with milk

Step 4

Cut bacons and onions into small pieces

Step 5

Prepare a pan with boiling water, then put salt and a few drops of olive oil into a pan

Step 6

Put 90g of spaghetti into boiling pan

Step 7

Put olive oil into a new pan. After the pan is heated, put chopped bacons and onions into a pan

Step 8

Put crunched peperoncinos into a pan. Stir until onion turns brown

Step 9

Put the mixture of milk and an egg yolk into a pan. Stir until it boils

Step 10

When spaghetti is ready, put spaghetti into the cream sauce and then stir so that the sauce is balanced throughout

Step 11

Put some parmesan cheese to finish the cream pasta. Stir a little, then you are done!
